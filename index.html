Frequently Asked Questions

Scientific answers on storage, analysis methods, synthesis, and handling for research peptides.

What is the recommended storage protocol for lyophilised peptides? +
Lyophilised peptides should be stored at −20 °C in a dry environment, protected from light and humidity. Avoid freeze-thaw cycling by preparing single-use aliquots before reconstitution. Bacteriostatic water (0.9% benzyl alcohol) is the preferred reconstitution solvent for extended stability post-reconstitution. Reconstituted aliquots stored at 4 °C remain stable for up to 30 days. Correctly stored lyophilised peptides maintain ≥95% purity for 24 months from the date of manufacture.
How is purity determined, and what HPLC method is used? +
Purity is quantified by reverse-phase HPLC (RP-HPLC) using a C18 column with a water/acetonitrile gradient (0.1% TFA modifier), with area-percentage integration at 220 nm UV. Molecular identity is confirmed by electrospray ionisation mass spectrometry (ESI-MS), comparing theoretical and observed m/z values to within ±0.1 Da. Full HPLC chromatograms and MS spectra are included in each lot's Certificate of Analysis.
What is the difference between acetate salt and TFA salt forms? +
The counterion in the final salt form affects aqueous solubility and biocompatibility. Trifluoroacetate (TFA) salts are the default product of Fmoc SPPS but carry cytotoxicity concerns at higher concentrations. Acetate is a physiological counterion making it preferred for biological research applications. All NeuroPep compounds undergo ion-exchange chromatography to yield acetate form as standard, with residual TFA content consistently below 0.1% by ¹⁹F NMR.
What are your minimum order quantities and packaging specifications? +
Research quantities start at 2 mg for standard peptides and 1 mg for complex or rare sequences. Products are packaged in amber borosilicate glass vials with lyophilised powder under nitrogen atmosphere. Each vial has a tamper-evident seal with lot number and QR code linking to the digital COA. Lyophilised peptides ship at ambient temperature. Reconstituted peptides are dispatched with dry ice. Discreet pharmaceutical-grade secondary packaging ensures product integrity in transit.
Do you offer custom peptide synthesis for research projects? +
Custom synthesis is available for sequences of 2–50 amino acids using Fmoc solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS) on automated synthesisers. Services include standard, modified (phosphorylated, biotinylated, fluorescently labelled), cyclic, and stapled peptides. Standard sequences require 10–15 business days. Competitive pricing is available for academic and institutional partners. Please contact the synthesis team with sequence, desired modifications, purity grade (crude / desalted / ≥95% / ≥98%), and required quantity.
